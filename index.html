Our Story

More than two decades of leather craftsmanship, carried into a new chapter.

YS Apparels carries forward more than 25 years of leather manufacturing heritage, originally established in 2000 as a small leather garment workshop with a handful of employees and limited production capacity.

Over the years that followed, the company expanded its capabilities, strengthened its quality systems, and built deep experience serving international buyers across Europe, the UK and the United States.

In 2026, the business began a new chapter under the YS Apparels name — backed by the same manufacturing expertise, updated management, and a sharpened focus on international markets. The identity is expressed through our slogan, "The 2nd Skin" — a focus on comfort, craftsmanship and fashion-led leather apparel.

From Concept to Shipment

One coordinated process, from raw material to export-ready collection.

A structured path with buyer approval built in at every critical stage — so what ships matches what was agreed.

01

Buyer inquiry & commercial review

Understanding the collection, volumes and commercial terms.

02

Design & tech-pack evaluation

Sketches, references or full tech packs reviewed for feasibility.

03

Material & trim selection

Leather, lining, hardware and trims matched to the brief.

04

Pattern & grading

Patterns built and graded across the required size range.

05

Sample making

Development, fitting and pre-production samples prepared.

06

Buyer approval

Fit, colour, construction and finish signed off before bulk.

07

Bulk production

Manufacturing proceeds against the approved specification.

08

Quality inspection

In-line and final checks against the buyer-approved sample.

09

Branding & packing

Labels, hangtags and packaging per buyer specification.

10

Export & shipment

Documentation and logistics coordinated per agreed Incoterms.
